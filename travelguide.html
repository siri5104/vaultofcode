The heart of the United Kingdom, London is a city of history, royalty, and timeless landmarks. Explore the regal Buckingham Palace, embrace the literary legacy of Shakespeare, and savor classic English tea in this cosmopolitan capital.
A land of diverse cultures, India captivates with its ancient history and vibrant traditions. From the majestic Taj Mahal to the serene backwaters of Kerala, India offers an enchanting blend of spirituality, flavors, and landscapes.
Australia, with its stunning landscapes and unique wildlife, is an adventurer's paradise. Dive into the Great Barrier Reef, trek through the Outback, and encounter koalas and kangaroos in this sun-kissed nation.
